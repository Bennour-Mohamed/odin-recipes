Spaghetti

A comforting baked spaghetti casserole with plenty of melted cheese is the perfect dish for potlucks, family gatherings, or a week-night dinner.

Ingredients

  • Package spaghetti
  • Beef
  • Onion, chopped
  • Eggs
  • cheeses

Steps

  1. Get a big enough pot and add enough water.
  2. Rolling boil.
  3. When your water is at a rolling boil, don't dump in the pasta just yet: First, add salt.
  4. Finally!
  5. Time to drain.
